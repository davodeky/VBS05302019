Today's Study
  • Psalm 18:1-7

Sunday's Sermon- GOD ANSWERS PRAYERS
  • Our God is good and is good regardless of wherever we stand.
  • I called onto the Lord- David was facing the torrents.
  • In my distress, challenging moments, my times of uncertainties, I called upon the Lord.
  • We sometimes get so used to what we may be going through that we do not make an effort to call upon the lord- we try to use our own strength.
  • Everybody needs the Lord.
  • Prayer is the secret thing that you do with your God, and surprisingly you see people find themselves doing things for you.
  • We want to find our own a place but we don’t want find our place in the secret place of the most high.
  • David was surrounded by death but he knew where to go.
  • Jesus wondered away in the evenings to go and pray- who are we not to pray?
  • Some of the attacks we get is because the enemy is after our destiny.
  • We act in fear because we don’t know how to position ourselves.
  • The veil was torn from down to the bottom- giving us access to the secret place of the most.

Study Questions
  • When you last faced challenges how did you talk about them?
  • What do we learn from David's approach to his challenges?
